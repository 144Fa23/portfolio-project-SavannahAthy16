Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a hard working college student with four years of design experience. I'm majoring in Digital Media: Content Strategy, and work as the Design Editor of the Missourian, a graphic design assistant at Northwest Missouri State University Marketing and am the owner of Breath of Life Design Studio.

Through web development and design, I aim to create new, innovative designs that improve the lives of others.

After graduation, I plan to work remotely so that I can travel the world and help people.

-My Story-

From the time I could do arts in crafts projects, to now being able to run adobe programs, I have been a creative person. Having the ability to create something beautiful out of nothing is one of the skills that makes me who I am.

My design experience is extremely versatile, ranging from physical drawings, newspaper page design, digital design, and now web design. I never knew that my dreams of being a journalist would one day turn me down the road of design, and furthermore to Northwest Missouri State University.
